## ğŸ”­ Technical Roadmap | From Control to Systems

ACRS views technology not as isolated projects, but as a cumulative system engineering trajectory.

We deliberately begin with **control systems**, and progressively advance toward higher-complexity domains:

- **Control Systems**  
  Establishing foundations in modeling, control theory, simulation, and experimental validation.

- **Robotic Systems**  
  Applying control to multi-DOF systems, integrating sensing, actuation, planning, and softwareâ€“hardware co-design.

- **Unmanned Systems (Aerial & Mobile)**  
  Extending into high-dynamics, nonlinear, and real-time systems such as UAVs and autonomous platforms.

- **Marine & Aerospace Systems**  
  Exploring, in the long term, complex control challenges in underwater vehicles, submarines, and rocket systems.

We do not rush toward the final destination.  
Each stage must produce **reproducible, transferable technical outcomes**, allowing future members to stand on solid foundations and move further.
